Fence Staining in Chicago

Keep your fence strong and beautiful through Chicago's toughest seasons.

Home› Services› Fence Staining

A wood fence is a significant investment — and Chicago's weather can destroy an unprotected one in just a few years. Professional staining adds both beauty and critical weather protection.

Primer Chicago cleans, brightens, and stains wood fences of all types: cedar, pine, redwood, and pressure-treated lumber. We apply penetrating stains that soak into the wood grain for maximum protection against moisture, UV, and freeze-thaw damage.

Whether your fence is brand new or needs restoration, we'll bring it back to life and keep it looking great for years to come.

Why Choose Primer Chicago for Fence Staining

  • All wood types: cedar, pine, redwood
  • Deep cleaning before staining
  • Penetrating stain application
  • UV and moisture protection
  • Gate and post coverage included

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

We recommend every 2-3 years for optimal protection. Fences that get heavy sun exposure or are in low-lying areas where moisture collects may need more frequent attention.
Painted fences can't be stained directly — the paint blocks penetration. We can strip the paint and then stain, or repaint the fence. We'll assess your fence and recommend the best approach.
We offer the full spectrum: natural/clear sealers that show the wood grain, semi-transparent stains that add color while showing grain, and solid stains that provide full color coverage like paint but with a softer finish.
