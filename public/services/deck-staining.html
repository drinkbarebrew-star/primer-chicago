Deck Staining in Chicago

Keep your deck beautiful and protected through every Chicago season.

Home› Services› Deck Staining

A Chicago deck takes serious punishment — ice, snow, rain, and UV rays. Without proper protection, wood decks can crack, splinter, and turn gray in just a couple of seasons.

Primer Chicago's deck staining services start with thorough cleaning and brightening. We remove old finish, kill mildew, and open the wood grain so the new stain penetrates deeply and lasts.

We offer transparent, semi-transparent, and solid stains from top brands like Cabot and TWP, chosen specifically for their performance in Midwest freeze-thaw conditions.

Why Choose Primer Chicago for Deck Staining

  • Deep cleaning and brightening
  • Old finish removal
  • Mildew treatment
  • Premium freeze-thaw rated stains
  • Railing and stair coverage included

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Most decks in Chicago need re-staining every 2-3 years due to the harsh freeze-thaw cycles. High-traffic areas may need attention sooner. We can assess your deck's condition for free.
We recommend semi-transparent penetrating stains for most Chicago decks. They absorb into the wood rather than sitting on top, which means they won't peel or crack when temperatures swing.
New pressure-treated lumber needs to weather for 3-6 months before staining to allow moisture to escape. We can assess your new deck and advise on the right timing.
