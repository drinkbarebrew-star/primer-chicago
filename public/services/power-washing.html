Power Washing in Chicago

Blast away years of grime from driveways, siding, patios, and more.

Home› Services› Power Washing

Chicago's weather leaves its mark — dirt, mildew, algae, and pollution build up on every exterior surface. Professional power washing removes years of buildup and restores surfaces to like-new condition.

Primer Chicago uses commercial-grade equipment with adjustable pressure for every surface type. We soft-wash delicate surfaces like painted wood and vinyl siding, and use higher pressure for concrete and brick.

Power washing is also the essential first step before any exterior painting or staining project. We include it with all exterior painting jobs and offer it as a standalone service for seasonal cleanups.
