Exterior Painting in Chicago

Weather-tough exterior painting that protects and transforms your Chicago home.

Home› Services› Exterior Painting

Chicago's weather is brutal on exteriors — freezing winters, humid summers, and everything in between. Primer Chicago's exterior painting services are designed to protect your home against the elements while keeping it looking sharp.

We start with a full exterior assessment: scraping loose paint, power washing surfaces, repairing damaged wood, and priming bare spots. Every project gets two coats of premium exterior paint rated for Midwest conditions.

From classic brick two-flats to modern siding, we have the equipment and expertise to handle any exterior — including multi-story homes requiring scaffolding.
