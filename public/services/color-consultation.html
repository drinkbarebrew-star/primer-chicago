Color Consultation in Chicago

Expert guidance to find the perfect palette for your home.

Home› Services› Color Consultation

Choosing paint colors is one of the most overwhelming parts of a painting project. With thousands of options and the way light, furnishings, and architecture interact, getting it right matters.

Primer Chicago offers professional color consultation to take the stress out of the decision. We consider your home's natural light, existing finishes, architectural style, and personal taste to recommend palettes that work.

We bring large-format samples to your home so you can see colors in your actual space — not under fluorescent lighting at a store. Our consultations are complimentary with any painting project.

Why Choose Primer Chicago for Color Consultation

  • In-home color evaluation
  • Large-format samples in your space
  • Coordination with existing finishes
  • Trend-aware recommendations
  • Complimentary with painting projects
