Wallpaper Installation in Chicago

Precision wallpaper installation for accent walls and full rooms.

Home› Services› Wallpaper Installation

Wallpaper is making a major comeback — from bold geometric patterns to subtle grasscloth textures. Proper installation is critical for a professional result that lasts.

Primer Chicago installs all types of wallpaper: peel-and-stick, pre-pasted, and traditional paste applications. We ensure walls are perfectly smooth and primed before installation, and every seam is tight and matched.

We also handle wallpaper removal, which can be one of the most tedious home improvement tasks. We steam and strip old wallpaper, repair any underlying damage, and prep the walls for fresh paper or paint.
