Plaster Repair in Chicago

Expert plaster restoration for Chicago's historic and classic homes.

Home› Services› Plaster Repair

Chicago is full of beautiful older homes with original plaster walls and ceilings. Over decades, plaster can crack, bubble, sag, or sustain water damage — but replacement isn't always the answer.

Primer Chicago specializes in plaster repair and restoration. We stabilize loose plaster, fill cracks, skim-coat uneven surfaces, and match existing textures so repairs blend seamlessly.

Whether it's hairline cracks in a Lincoln Park greystone or water-damaged ceilings in a vintage Bucktown two-flat, we have the skills and materials to restore your plaster to its original condition.

Why Choose Primer Chicago for Plaster Repair

  • Crack and hole repair
  • Skim coating for smooth finishes
  • Texture matching
  • Water damage restoration
  • Historic plaster preservation
