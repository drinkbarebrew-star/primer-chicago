About Primer Chicago

Locally owned. Quality driven. Serving Chicago neighborhoods with pride.

Our Story

Primer Chicago was founded with a simple belief: homeowners deserve painting services that combine expert craftsmanship with honest, transparent business practices. Too many painting companies cut corners on prep, use cheap materials, or leave customers guessing about timelines and costs.

We do things differently. Every project starts with a thorough assessment and a detailed, written estimate — no surprises, no hidden fees. We use premium paints from Benjamin Moore and Sherwin-Williams because they cover better, last longer, and look richer than budget alternatives.

Our crews are trained professionals, not day laborers. They prep meticulously, paint precisely, and clean up thoroughly. We treat your home the way we'd want ours treated — with care, respect, and attention to every detail.
